Summary

We look for a model to predict the activity did by our users, and to know how well they do it. After to have clean the dataset, we checked some differents methods and finished with a random forest that it gave us the best result.

Study

We clean the dataset, we remove all columns with amplitude, standard deviation, average, variation, minimum, maximum, kurtosis and skewness because it are useless for the study, a lot of NA and it are simply agregation of the others variables. So we get a data frame of 53 columns, because we remove to the seven fisrt columns, which reprensent the subject variable and time.
